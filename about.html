About us

Welcome to FEC-PR

FEC PR Global Services is dedicated to fostering a culture of Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) that prioritizes the creation of value for all stakeholders, including customers, employees, suppliers, the environment, and shareholders. The company is committed to developing sustainable practices, particularly in the context of its operations in the tannery sector.

At FEC PR Global Services, employees are not just workers; they are integral members of the community. The company encourages its staff to actively participate in community engagement initiatives, such as constructing footbridges in eastern Nigeria and partnering with organizations like Habitat for Humanity to build homes. This involvement instills a sense of pride and purpose among employees, making their work more meaningful and impactful.

The company's guiding principle, encapsulated in its motto “Reliability is Our Strength,” underscores its commitment to conducting business with the highest standards of integrity and ethics. FEC PR Global Services believes that ethical business practices are essential to building trust and long-lasting relationships with all stakeholders.

Innovation and development are also core values at FEC PR Global Services. The company is focused on fostering creativity and the generation of new ideas, which are crucial for staying competitive and meeting the evolving needs of its clients and the market. By prioritizing these values, FEC PR Global Services aims to not only achieve business success but also contribute positively to society and the environment
